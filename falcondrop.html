About Me

My name is Tawonga "Pure Yeet" Banda - and this is Falcon Drop.I don't follow trends I drop verdicts.

I hold a Graphic Design Diploma from MacMaine School of Computing under Businesss and Computing Examinations London - an internationally recognised qualification. I am currently expanding my skills by studying Web Design at the same institution, and this very site is proof of that dedication. I also hold a Certificate in Fine Art from the National Art Gallery of Zimbabwe - because the creative eye never sleeps.

Proudly repping Zimbabwe, my passions run deep — gaming, football, chaos art, and animated cinema. These aren't just hobbies, they're obsessions. Falcon Drop exists because these worlds deserve brutal honesty, sharp analysis, and real opinions — not recycled hype. Every post is written with research, passion, and absolutely zero filter.
