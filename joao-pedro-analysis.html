Joao Pedro — Most Improved — The Transformation is Real

Club: Chelsea FC
Season: 2024/25
Reviewed by: Pure Yeet
8.7
/ 10 Pure Yeet Verdict
Chelsea's Best Signing This Season. No Debate.
Screamers. Worldies. Drogba vibes. The transformation is real.

Nobody Saw This Coming

At the start of the season nobody was talking about Joao Pedro as Chelsea's most important player. He had disciplinary issues. He looked soft. He was going down looking for easy fouls instead of putting the ball in the net. Good link up play — yes. But soft. That was the word around him early on.

Fast forward to now and this man is scoring screamers and worldies and having some of the best individual performances in Europe. The transformation is one of the most impressive stories of the entire season and Pure Yeet is here to give him his flowers. 🌸

From Soft to Drogba — The Transformation

The early Joao Pedro at Chelsea was frustrating to watch as a fan. You could see the quality was there — the touch, the movement, the link up play was genuinely good. But he was soft. Going down for fouls instead of finishing. Not putting his body on the line. Not imposing himself.

And then something clicked. Suddenly this is a man scoring goals that remind you of Didier Drogba in his prime — powerful, clinical, unstoppable. The physicality came. The ruthlessness came. The goals came. And they did not stop coming.

⚽ The Napoli Away Game — That Was The Moment

Away to Napoli. European football. Big stage. Joao Pedro showed up and delivered one of his best individual performances. Brilliant goals that secured Chelsea's place in the last 16 — no play offs needed. That night told you everything about where his confidence was.

The Napoli Night — No Play Offs Needed

The away game at Napoli was the moment that really showed what Joao Pedro had become. European football, hostile atmosphere, a game Chelsea needed to win — and he delivered. Brilliant goals that secured Chelsea in the last 16 without needing play offs.

That is the kind of performance that defines a player's season. Big stage. Big moment. Big goals. Joao Pedro did not flinch. That is not the same soft player from the start of the season — that is a player who has found himself completely.

Screamers and Worldies — The Goals Are Special

Let's talk about the actual goals because they deserve their own section. Joao Pedro is not just scoring tap ins — this man is hitting screamers from distance, finishing with power, and producing worldies that make you rewatch the clip three times. The quality of his goals this season has been something else.

The Drogba comparison is not made lightly. Drogba was a player who could win a game on his own through sheer individual brilliance and physical dominance. When Joao Pedro is in this form he carries that same energy. Chelsea fans know exactly what that means.

Final Verdict

🟢 PURE YEET VERDICT — 8.7 / 10

Joao Pedro is Chelsea's most improved player and best signing of this season. Nobody saw this coming. He started soft, had disciplinary issues, was looking for easy fouls instead of goals. And then he flipped the switch completely. Screamers. Worldies. Drogba vibes. That Napoli away performance where he scored brilliant goals to put us in the last 16 with no play offs needed — that was the night the whole world saw what Chelsea had on their hands. The transformation is real and Pure Yeet is giving him full credit. 🦅🔵
