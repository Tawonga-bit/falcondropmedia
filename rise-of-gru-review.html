🎞️ Animated Film Review

Minions: The Rise of Gru

Studio: Illumination
Year: 2022
Reviewed by: Pure Yeet
8.2
/ 10 Pure Yeet Verdict
A Mini Villain. A Massive Vibe.
Origin story filled with pure chaotic energy

Origin Story Perfection

Rise of Gru fills in the gaps we didn't know we needed. A tiny kid who wants to be the most feared villain in the world—it’s hilarious and surprisingly rooted in a great character arc.

😂 Pure Yeet's Highlight

Those three Minions defeating bad guys while dressed as a chicken and a bunny. Comedy gold that nobody saw coming.

The Mini Villain Concept

Young Gru has the accent, the ambition, and zero respect from the "pros." Watching him navigate a room full of supervillains and refusing to be intimidated is the heart of the movie.

+ WHAT WORKS
  • Mini villain premise is original
  • Chicken/Bunny sequence is iconic
  • Retired villains as heroes is a twist
  • Wild Knuckles is an S-Tier character
  • Perfect origin story bridge
− WHAT DOESN'T
  • Criminally underrated by audiences
  • Pacing feels a bit rushed near the end

Final Verdict

🟠 PURE YEET VERDICT — 8.2 / 10

Goofy, creative, and way more entertaining than critics gave it credit for. It captures the chaos of the Minions while giving Gru a legitimate reason to be who he becomes. Pure Yeet has spoken. 🦅
