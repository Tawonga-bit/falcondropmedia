Will Arteta Finally Do It?

Season: 2025/26
Arsenal Points: 67
Gap: 7 Points Clear
Reviewed by: Pure Yeet
50/50
Pure Yeet Verdict
It Depends On City. Simple As That.
As a Chelsea fan I am ready to hear the noise 😂

Three Seasons. Second Place. The Memes Are Real.

Let me start by acknowledging the elephant in the room — the Arteta memes. Three seasons in a row finishing second. Three seasons of coming so close and watching someone else lift the trophy. The internet has not been kind to Arsenal and honestly? The memes have been accurate.

But here is the thing — Arsenal are now seven points clear at the top of the Premier League. Arteta has built something real at that club and this might genuinely be the season where the memes finally retire. Pure Yeet is watching closely. 👀

Man City — The Circus That Made Klopp Quit

Before we talk about Arsenal we have to talk about Man City — because they are the reason this conversation is even complicated. For years City turned the Premier League into their personal trophy cabinet. Every. Single. Year. 😂

And the most wild part? They made Jurgen Klopp quit Liverpool. One of the greatest managers in football history decided he did not have the energy to keep fighting the machine. That is the hurdle Arsenal has to clear.

😂 The Award Circus

Manager of the Season — Pep. Player of the Season — City player. Golden Boot — City striker. Young Player — Foden. Every year. City turned the Premier League into a one-team show.

Arsenal Must Prove It In The EFL Cup First

Here is Pure Yeet's key point — before Arsenal can be taken seriously as title winners they need to prove it in the EFL Cup final first. That is silverware. That is a statement that this squad knows how to win when it matters most.

+ ARSENAL CAN WIN IF
  • They win the EFL Cup to build momentum
  • Saka and Odegaard stay fit and firing
  • They keep attacking and don't park the bus
  • City continue their inconsistent form
− ARSENAL WILL BOTTLE IF
  • City go on a classic Pep winning run
  • Key injuries hit at the wrong time
  • They start protecting the lead too early
  • The pressure of history gets in their heads
🟢 PURE YEET VERDICT — 50/50

Arsenal are seven points clear and Arteta has built something genuinely special. The problem is Man City. You cannot count them out until it is mathematically impossible. Arsenal must win the EFL Cup, keep believing, and stay focused. As a Chelsea fan, I have no dog in this fight — but I'm ready for the noise. Pure Yeet has spoken. 🦅
